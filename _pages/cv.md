## Profile

Senior researcher with over 25 years in fundamental and applied research on polymer materials and surface engineering, and proven leadership of EU-funded projects. Project Director of TalentPass (Horizon Europe) and POINGBIO (ERDF), with hands-on experience across the main EU funding instruments — Horizon Europe, Framework Programmes (FP6), ERDF, and ESF — covering proposal development, consortium building, budgeting, implementation, and reporting through the EU Funding & Tenders Portal. Strong scientific record (100+ Web of Science articles, h-index 24, ~1,700 citations, 4 patents) and consistent experience in scientific peer review and in regional Smart Specialisation (RIS3) evaluation bodies. Project-management certified.

**At a glance:** €18M+ EU funding led/co-managed · 100+ Web of Science articles · h-index 24 · 4 patents (+2 pending)
