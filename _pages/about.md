I am a Senior Researcher (Grade I) at the "Petru Poni" Institute of Macromolecular Chemistry of the Romanian Academy in Iași, where I lead a research group on the physico-chemical properties of structured polymer materials. I hold a PhD in Physics and a Habilitation in Chemistry, and I have spent more than 25 years working at the interface of polymer physics, surface engineering, and biomaterials.

My research centres on advanced and nanostructured polymer materials and on how their surfaces can be modified — through plasma, chemical, UV, and laser treatments — to create biocompatible and antimicrobial surfaces for medical devices such as catheters, implants, and wound dressings. I also work on nanocomposites and nanoparticles (silver, gold, iron oxides), electrospinning, and materials for packaging, electromagnetic shielding, and the circular economy, supported by structural characterisation through X-ray diffraction (XRD/PXRD) and vibrational spectroscopy (RAMAN, FTIR).

Alongside the science, I coordinate and manage EU-funded research. I am the Project Coordinator of **TalentPass** (Horizon Europe, 2025–2029), a Widening/Talents action on circular-economy capacity building, and I directed **POINGBIO** (ERDF, 2016–2022), a research-to-industry knowledge-transfer partnership in biomedical polymer materials. Across my career I have led or co-managed more than €18 million in EU funding and taken part in over ten national and international projects spanning Horizon Europe, the Framework Programmes, ERDF, and ESF.

I am an active peer reviewer for Elsevier, Wiley, MDPI, and CRC Press, a guest editor and book editor, a certified project manager, and a member of regional Smart Specialisation (RIS3) evaluation bodies in North-East Romania. I currently supervise doctoral candidates and am open to collaboration as well as to project-coordination and evaluation roles.

## Research interests

- Polymer physics and chemistry; advanced and nanostructured polymer materials
- Surface modification and functionalisation (plasma, chemical, UV, and laser)
- Biomaterials; biocompatible and antimicrobial surfaces
- Nanocomposites and nanoparticles (Ag, Au, iron oxides); electrospinning
- Green synthesis, recycling, and circular-economy materials
- Structural characterisation by XRD/PXRD and RAMAN/FTIR spectroscopy
