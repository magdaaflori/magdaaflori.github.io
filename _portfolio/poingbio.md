**POINGBIO — Knowledge Transfer Partnerships in biomedical polymer materials** was a research-to-industry knowledge-transfer partnership that I directed, bridging fundamental polymer research and the medical-device industry.

- **Role:** Project Director
- **Programme:** Operational Programme Competitiveness 2014–2020 (ERDF), Action 1.2.3
- **Project codes:** P_40_443, SMIS 105689
- **Duration:** 2016–2022
- **Total budget:** ~€3.2 million (RON 15.8M)

The partnership transferred know-how on biocompatible and antimicrobial polymer surfaces — for applications such as catheters, implants, and wound dressings — from the laboratory to industrial partners.
