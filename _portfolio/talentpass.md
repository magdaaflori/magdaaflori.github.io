**TalentPass — Fostering European Talents for Widening Circular Economy** is a Horizon Europe Widening/Talents action focused on circular-economy capacity building, which I coordinate.

- **Role:** Project Coordinator
- **Programme:** Horizon Europe — HORIZON-WIDERA-2024-TALENTS-03
- **Funding body:** European Research Executive Agency (REA)
- **Grant agreement:** 101217448
- **Duration:** 2025–2029
- **Total budget:** ~€3 million

The project develops research talent and institutional capacity around circular-economy materials and practices, strengthening the participation of widening countries in the European Research Area.
